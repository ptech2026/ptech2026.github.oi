Our Story

Druk Basket was founded with a simple goal: to bridge the gap between local producers and the modern marketplace. We believe that Bhutan's unique agricultural heritage—from GI Tagged Red Rice to Certified Organic vegetables—deserves to be accessible to everyone.

🌱 100% Organic

Prioritizing health by promoting products free from synthetic chemicals.

🤝 Fair Trade

Ensuring our farmers receive the best market prices for their hard work.

🏔️ Sustainable

Supporting traditional farming methods that protect Bhutan's environment.
